United States Visa Appointment Info

Select a visa type below to learn more about the requirements and schedule your appointment.

F1 / F2 Visas

F1: For students enrolled in academic programs at U.S. institutions.
F2: For dependents (spouse or children) of F1 visa holders.

B1 / B2 Visas

B1: For business-related visits, including conferences or meetings.
B2: For tourism, visiting family, or medical treatment.

H1B / H4 Visas

H1B: For professionals in specialty occupations.
H4: For dependents (spouse or children) of H1B holders.

Other Visas

L1: For intra-company transferees working in the U.S.
J1: For individuals in educational and cultural exchange programs.
